# Product Requirements Document: The Procrastination ROI Calculator

## 1. Product Overview
* **Product Name:** ProcrastinCalc (or "Just Do It")
* **Core Philosophy:** Why spend 10 minutes doing a chore when you can spend 4 hours automating it?
* **Primary Goal:** A satirical web app that takes a mundane physical task, calculates the theoretical time and cost to over-engineer a software solution for it, and ruthlessly roasts the user for avoiding their life.

## 2. Core Experience & Features
* **The Input:** A giant, brutalist hero section with two inputs: 
  1. "What simple task are you actively avoiding?" (String)
  2. "How many minutes would it take to just do it manually?" (Integer)
* **The Loading State:** Fake loading messages that cycle through developer tropes (e.g., "Arguing with Cursor...", "Bootstrapping Next.js...", "Abstracting the socks array...").
* **The Output (AI Generated):** * **The Over-engineered Stack:** A ridiculous, unnecessarily complex tech stack to solve the simple problem (e.g., Kubernetes + Kafka to fold hoodies).
  * **Automation Time:** A massively inflated estimated time to build the app (in hours).
  * **Net Time Wasted:** The mathematical difference between manual execution and automation.
  * **The Verdict:** A sarcastic, brutally honest roast from the AI telling the user to close their laptop.

## 3. Design Aesthetic
* **Vibe:** Terminal/Hacker-chic meets brutalist SaaS. Monospace fonts, dark mode (`#050505` background), neon green accents (`#00FF00`) for "Manual" and blood red (`#FF0000`) for "Automation".
